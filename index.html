Flock Simulation

There are 3 rules to flock behavior:
  1. you do not talk about flock behavior!
just kidding ;) rules are:
  1. Separation - avoid crowding neighbors (short range repulsion)
  2. Alignment - steer towards average heading of neighbors
  3. Cohesion - steer towards average position of neighbors (long range attraction)

Source: Wikipedia

In this siumulation, you can controle the radius of each of the 3 parameter above using the slides, and watch how the flock change. You can also turn on/off each of the rule radius for better visability.

Flock simulation was created in ClojureScript, base on the Mondrian lib .
